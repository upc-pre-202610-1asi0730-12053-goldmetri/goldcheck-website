Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

GoldMetrics is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use and safeguard your personal information when you use the GoldCheck platform.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly (name, email, company details) and data generated through platform usage (traceability records, verification logs, analytics).

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to provide traceability services, generate certifications, improve platform functionality and communicate important updates. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encryption, access controls and regular audits to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration or destruction.

4. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct or delete your personal data at any time. You may also request a copy of the data we hold about you by contacting our support team.

5. Cookies & Tracking

GoldCheck uses essential cookies to maintain your session and language preferences. We do not use third-party tracking cookies for advertising purposes.

6. Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at privacy@goldmetrics.com.
